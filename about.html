About Waha Al Faisal Trading

Waha Al Faisal Trading is a trusted wholesale supplier of premium home decoration products, backed by over 25 years of industry experience. Over the years, we have developed strong partnerships with leading manufacturers in China, enabling us to supply high-quality decorative products to retailers, distributors, and businesses.

We collaborate with experienced factory owners who prioritize craftsmanship and quality over mass production, ensuring that every product meets high standards. Along with our ready-made product range, we also offer custom manufacturing directly from factories, allowing clients to develop unique products tailored to their brand and market needs.

To support international buyers, we offer various shipping solutions, including efficient warehouse-to-warehouse shipping options. We are also partnered with highly experienced customs agents, allowing us to provide reliable shipping estimates and smooth customs clearance support for imported goods.

At Waha Al Faisal Trading, our goal is to deliver quality products, dependable manufacturing partnerships, and streamlined global sourcing solutions for businesses seeking premium home decoration items.
